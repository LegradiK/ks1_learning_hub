How to Play

🖼 Beginner Mode

Look at the picture clues on the side. Each picture is a word — can you spell it?

  • Find the number on the grid that matches the picture
  • Click that square and type the letters to spell the word
  • Words go Across → or Down ↓
  • Press ✅ Check to see if you got it right

📚 Advanced Mode

Read the clues on the side and work out the answer.

  • Find the matching number on the grid
  • Click and type your answer letter by letter
  • Words go Across → or Down ↓
  • The number of letters is shown under each clue

🎮 Buttons

  • 🎲 New puzzle — load a fresh puzzle
  • ✅ Check — mark right and wrong letters
  • 👁 Reveal — show all the answers
  • 🗑 Clear — wipe the grid and start again
