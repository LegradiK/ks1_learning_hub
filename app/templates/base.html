🔤 Letter Quest

A crossword made just for you! Solve the clues and fill the grid with words.

🖼 Beginner Mode

Every clue is a picture. Say what you see, then spell it!

  • Look at a picture and find its number on the grid
  • Tap that square and type the word, one letter in each box
  • Words go Across → or Down ↓ — follow the boxes!

📚 Advanced Mode

Ready for a challenge? Now the clues are riddles to read.

  • Read a clue and work out the mystery word
  • The little number under each clue tells you how many letters it has
  • Stuck on one? Skip it — crossing words will give you free letters!

🎮 Buttons

  • 🎲 New puzzle — shuffle up a brand new grid
  • ✅ Check — green means right, red means try again
  • 👁 Reveal — peek at all the answers
  • 🗑 Clear — wipe everything and have another go

🔢 Math Drill

One sum at a time — how many can you get right in a row?

  • Pick a sum type: ➕ ➖ ✖️ or ➗
  • Some types let you choose how tricky the sums are — start easy and level up!
  • Type your answer and press ✅ Check (or hit Enter)
  • Every right answer grows your 🔥 streak — one wrong answer and it's back to zero!
  • Sum too tough? 🔁 Skip swaps it. 🧹 Start again wipes your score for a fresh run

Word Wizard

Listen like a wizard, spell like a champion!

  • Pick a stage: tricky words, vocabulary, top words — or fill in the missing word in a sentence
  • Choose your level: 🌱 Reception, 📗 Year 1, 📘 Year 2 — or All, which mixes every word from Reception to Year 2
  • Press 🔊 Listen to hear your word — you can listen as many times as you like
  • Type your spelling, then click 👀 Reveal or press Enter to see how you did, letter by letter
  • Right answers build your 🔥 streak — and no word repeats until you've beaten them all!

🧩 Sudoku

A puzzle of logic — no adding up needed, just clever thinking!

🎯 The rules

  • Every row going across needs each number exactly once
  • Every column going down needs each number too
  • And so does every box (the areas with thick borders)
  • On a 4×4 grid that's the numbers 1 to 4, on 6×6 it's 1 to 6, and on the big 9×9 it's 1 to 9
  • The shaded numbers are clues to start you off — they can't be changed

✏️ How to fill it in

  • Tap any empty square — its row and column light up to help you check for repeats
  • Tap a number on the number pad to fill the square, or type it on a keyboard
  • Made a mistake? Press Erase (or Backspace) to rub it out
  • Use the arrow keys to hop between squares
  • Top tip: find a row, column or box that's nearly full — only one number can fit in the gap!

🎮 Buttons & extras

  • Filled every square? Press ✅ Check or hit Enter — green squares are right, red ones need another look
  • Stuck on a square? Tap it, then press 💡 Reveal — the answer appears in purple so you can see which ones you had help with
  • 🐾 Animals mode swaps numbers for animals — then every row needs one of each animal instead!
  • Pick your challenge: grid size (4×4 → 9×9) and level (🌱 Easy → 🌳 Hard) — fewer clues means more thinking
  • 🎲 New game deals a fresh puzzle; 🔄 Restart clears your answers but keeps the clues

💰 Money Counter

Get to know your pennies and pounds!

🔍 Count it

  • Look at the money on the screen and add it all up
  • Type the total — like 72p or £1.50 — then press ✅ Check or hit Enter

🛒 Make it

  • Look at the amount you need to make
  • Tap money in the tray to pop it into your purse
  • Tap a money in your purse to take it back out
  • Watch your purse total grow, then press ✅ Check when it matches!
  • Any coins and notes that add up right are correct — there's more than one way to make most amounts

⭐ Levels

  • 🌱 Easy — little coins: 1p, 2p, 5p and 10p
  • 🌿 Medium — adds the seven-sided 20p and 50p
  • 🌳 Hard — adds the gold £1 and £2 coins
  • 💷 Expert — banknotes! £5, £10, £20 and £50
  • Right answers build your 🔥 streak — press 🔁 New question anytime for a fresh one

⏰ Clock Master

Learn to tell the time — first read the clock, then set it yourself!

👀 Read it

  • Look at the clock — the short hand tells you the hour, the long hand tells you the minutes
  • Work out the time, then tap the right answer from the choices below
  • Picked wrong? Look at the hands again and have another go!

🕹 Set it

  • Read the time you need to make — like half past 3
  • Drag the hands around the clock: the short one to the hour, the long one to the minutes
  • The hands click into place as you move them — press ✅ Check when your clock looks right!

⭐ Levels

  • 🌱 O'clock — times like 3 o'clock, just the hour hand to think about
  • 🌿 Half past — the long hand points straight down at the 6
  • 🌳 Quarters — quarter past and quarter to join in
  • 🔥 5 minutes — any time on the clock, like twenty-five past 7!
  • Right answers build your 🔥 streak — press 🔁 New question anytime

🃏 Match Pairs

A memory game — find each picture's word hiding on the board!

🎯 How to play

  • All the cards start face down. Tap a card to flip it over
  • Then tap a second card — you're looking for a picture and its word, like 🐱 and cat
  • If they match, you take the pair and it disappears from the board — listen for the word!
  • If they don't match, take a good look before they flip back — remember where they are for later!
  • Match every pair to win

⭐ Levels & scoring

  • 🌱 Easy — 4 pairs with little words like cat and sun
  • 🌿 Medium — 6 pairs, slightly trickier words
  • 🌳 Hard — 10 pairs to remember
  • 🔥 Extra hard — a whopping 16 pairs! Play with a grown-up and see who remembers best
  • Your 🔄 flips are counted — fewer flips means a sharper memory. Can you beat your 🏆 best score?

🦊 Phonics Fox

Follow the fox and hunt for sounds — hear them, spot them, spell them!

🧩 Missing sound

  • Press 🔊 Listen to hear the word, then look at it — some letters are missing!
  • Work out which sound fills the gap — like ai in r__n
  • Tap the right sound and watch the word complete itself

👀 Pick the word

  • Listen to the word, then look closely — only one spelling is right!
  • The tricksters look almost real (rain… rayn… rane…) so sound each one out
  • Tap the true spelling — picked a trickster? Listen again and have another go

✏️ Spell it

  • Listen carefully — you can press 🔊 as many times as you like
  • Type the whole word, then press ✅ Check or hit Enter
  • The letter tiles show exactly which letters to fix — it's usually the tricky sound!

⭐ Year groups (for grown-ups)

  • 🌱 Reception — Phases 2 to 4: first sounds like ck, ll and ss, the digraphs sh, ch, th and ng, vowel sounds like ai, ee, igh and oa, and trickier ones like ear and air. Words include blends to practise (train, string, crown)
  • 📗 Year 1 — Phase 5: new spellings for sounds they already know — ay, ea, ou, ph, wh and friends — plus the "magic e" split digraphs (a‑e in cake, i‑e in kite). Flying through phonics early? This is the button for late-Reception Phase 5 work too
  • 📘 Year 2 — spelling rules: silent letters (kn, wr, gn), soft c (city), dge (bridge), tion (station) and -le endings (table)

👨/👩 Pick the voice

  • Pick a Man or Woman voice, build your 🔥 streak, and press 🔁 for a new word anytime

🔍 Story Detective

Some words have gone missing — can you crack the case?

🔍How to Play

Sneaky gaps have appeared in this story, and only you can fix it.

  • 📖Read the whole story first — a good detective gathers all the clues before deciding.
  • 🖱️Drag a word from the clue bank into a gap, or tap the word to drop it into the next empty gap.
  • Changed your mind? Tap a filled gap to take the word back.
  • ✋Watch out — some words look right but don't match the story!
  • When every gap is full, press Check✅ my story to see if you've cracked the case.
